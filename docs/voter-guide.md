# AGM Voting — Voter Guide

This guide explains how to cast your vote at an Annual General Meeting (AGM) using the online voting portal.

---

## Before you start

You will need:
- The email address registered with your building manager
- Access to that email inbox (to receive a verification code)

You do not need to create an account or install anything.

---

## Step 1 — Find your meeting

1. Open the voting portal link provided by your building manager.
2. Type your building name into the search box and select it from the results.
3. Find your meeting in the list.

A meeting can have one of three statuses:

| Status | What you see | What it means |
|---|---|---|
| Not yet open | "Voting Not Yet Open" (greyed out) | Voting hasn't started — check back later |
| Open | "Enter Voting" (clickable) | You can vote now |
| Closed | "View My Submission" | Voting has ended — you can review what you submitted |

Click **Enter Voting** to begin.

![Home page showing building search box and meeting list with Enter Voting button](screenshots/01-home.png)

---

## Step 2 — Verify your identity

Voting uses a one-time code sent to your email — no password required.

1. Enter your registered email address and click **Send Verification Code**.

![Auth step 1 — enter your email address](screenshots/02-auth-email.png)

2. Check your inbox for an email with an 8-character code (e.g. `A3BX92KL`).
   - The code expires in **5 minutes**.
   - If you don't see it, check your spam/junk folder.
3. Enter the code and click **Verify**.

![Auth step 2 — enter the verification code](screenshots/03-auth-code.png)

> **Already voted recently?** If you return to the portal within 30 minutes, you will be taken straight to voting without needing to verify again.

---

## Step 3 — Select your lots (if you own or represent multiple lots)

If you are associated with more than one lot, you will see a **Your Lots** panel on the left side of the screen (or tap **☰ Your Lots** on mobile to open it).

![Lot selection sidebar showing checkboxes for each lot](screenshots/03-lots.png)

- Tick the lots you want to vote for in this session.
- Use the shortcut buttons to quickly adjust your selection:
  - **Select All** — tick all lots that haven't been submitted yet
  - **Deselect All** — untick all lots
  - **Select Proxy Lots** — tick only proxy lots (shown when you have both owned and proxy lots)
  - **Select Owned Lots** — tick only your own lots
- You can submit votes for some lots now and return later to vote for the rest.
- Lots already submitted are shown as **Already submitted** and cannot be changed.
- The **View Submission** button appears in the panel as soon as any lot has been submitted.

---

## Step 4 — Cast your votes

Each motion is listed as a card on the voting screen. Each card shows the motion number, a **General** or **Special** badge, and the motion title.

A progress bar at the top shows how many motions you have answered.

### Standard (binary) motions

For each motion, choose one of:

- **For** — you support the motion
- **Against** — you oppose the motion
- **Abstained** — you choose not to vote on this motion

You can change your selection at any time before submitting.

![Voting page showing motion cards with For / Against / Abstained options](screenshots/04-voting.png)

### Multi-choice motions

Some motions present a list of options instead of a single For/Against choice. These are marked with a **Multi-Choice** badge.

For each option in the list, choose one of **For**, **Against**, or **Abstain**. A counter at the top of the list shows how many options you have voted For and the maximum allowed.

![Multi-choice motion card showing individual options with For / Against / Abstain buttons](screenshots/04-multi-choice.png)

### Per-motion voting windows

Occasionally a motion may close before the overall meeting closes. A **Motion Closed** badge will appear on that card and its buttons will be disabled. Any motions that close before you answer them are excluded from your progress bar.

### If your lot is in arrears

If your lot has an outstanding financial obligation to the building, it will be marked **In Arrear**.

- You can still vote on **Special Motions** — your vote will be counted normally.
- On **General Motions**, your vote will be recorded as **Not eligible** regardless of your selection.

A notice at the top of the page will explain this if it applies to you.

![Voting page with in-arrear warning banner and In Arrear badge on lot](screenshots/06-arrear.png)

### Voting countdown

The voting screen shows a countdown timer. When less than 5 minutes remain, a warning banner will appear. Once the timer reaches zero, voting closes and you will not be able to submit.

---

## Step 5 — Submit your ballot

When you are ready to submit:

1. Click **Submit ballot**.
2. If you have selected lots with different prior vote histories, a warning dialog will appear — review it and click **Continue** to proceed or **Go back** to adjust your selection.
3. A confirmation dialog will appear.
   - If any motions were left unanswered, they will be listed — they will be recorded as **Abstained** if you proceed.
4. Click **Submit ballot** to confirm.

> **Your votes cannot be changed after submission.**

---

## Step 6 — Confirmation

After submitting you will see a confirmation screen showing:

- The building and meeting name
- Your email address
- Each motion and the vote you cast for each lot (for multi-choice motions, each option's choice is shown individually)

You can return to this page at any time (even after the meeting closes) by clicking **View My Submission** on the home page.

![Confirmation page showing submitted votes for each motion](screenshots/05-confirmation.png)

---

## Proxy voting

If someone has nominated you as their proxy, their lots will appear in your lot list marked **via Proxy**. You vote on their behalf by selecting those lots and submitting votes as normal.

If you have both your own lots and proxy lots, use the **Select Proxy Lots** and **Select Owned Lots** shortcut buttons to switch between them.

![Lot selection showing owned lot and proxy lot with via Proxy badge](screenshots/07-proxy.png)

---

## Signing out

To end your session, click **Sign out** at the top of the voting page. This invalidates your session immediately on the server and returns you to the home page.

---

## Common issues

**"Email address not found for this building"**
Your email address is not registered with this building. Contact your building manager to confirm the email on file.

**"Invalid or expired code"**
The verification code has expired (they last 5 minutes) or was entered incorrectly. Click **Resend code** to get a new one. You must wait 60 seconds between resend requests.

**Voting closes while you are on the page**
A banner will appear and all vote buttons will be disabled. If you have not submitted yet, your votes have not been recorded — contact your building manager.

**You return after submitting some lots**
You will be taken to the confirmation page. Click **Vote for remaining lots** to continue voting for any lots you have not yet submitted.

**The portal shows "Voting Not Yet Open"**
Voting has not started for this meeting. Check with your building manager for the scheduled time.

**A motion card shows "Motion Closed"**
That motion's individual voting window has ended. You cannot vote on it — it will not count against your unanswered total.
